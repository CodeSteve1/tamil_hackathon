Historical and Political Overview

The Chola dynasty is one of the most eminent and long-ruling Tamil empires in Indian history. Starting around the 9th century CE, their prowess in maritime trade, military organization, and administrative reforms allowed them to expand their influence over India, Sri Lanka, and parts of Southeast Asia. Emperors like Raja Raja Chola I and Rajendra Chola played a significant role in elevating this empire.

Art, Architecture, and Literature

The Chola period is renowned for its magnificent temples like the Brihadeeswarar Temple and Gangaikonda Cholapuram. This era saw a flourishing of temple architecture that influenced later developments in South India. Accomplished poets and scholars also enriched Tamil literature during this time.

Economy and Administration

Under Chola rule, both maritime and inland trade thrived, bolstered by efficient administration and advanced technologies in construction and governance. Their systematic policies contributed greatly to the empireтАЩs prosperity and stability.

Key Emperors: Raja Raja Chola I, Rajendra Chola, among others.
